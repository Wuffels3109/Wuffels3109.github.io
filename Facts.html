Facts About Pizza

Ancient Roots: The concept of flatbreads topped with various ingredients dates back to ancient civilizations, including the Greeks, Romans, and Egyptians. The Etruscans, who lived in modern-day Italy, were among the first to enjoy pizza-like flatbreads. Birthplace of Modern Pizza: The modern pizza we know today originated in Naples, Italy, during the 18th century, where it was initially a simple dish for the working class.
