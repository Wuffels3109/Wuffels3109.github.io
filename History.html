The History of Pizza

The word "Pizza" was first documented in 997 AD in Italy, derived from the Latin word "pinsa," meaning "to flatten out". The easy answer would be that pizza was invented in Naples, Italy, by Raffaele Esposito in the 19th century CE. When King Umberto and Queen Margherita visited Naples in 1889, Esposito made the first premier pizzas in the world for the monarchs.
